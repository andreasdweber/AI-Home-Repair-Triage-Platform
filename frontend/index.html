This is a demo page showing the embeddable Fix-It AI widget. Click the blue chat button in the bottom-right corner to open the widget.

The widget uses Shadow DOM for complete CSS isolation - it won't affect your site's styles, and your styles won't affect it.

Embed on Your Site

Add this snippet to any page:

<!-- Fix-It AI Widget -->
<div id="fixit-widget"></div>
<script src="https://your-domain.com/fixit-widget.js"></script>

<!-- Or with custom config -->
<script>
  window.FixItAI.mountWidget('fixit-widget', {
    apiUrl: 'https://your-api.com',
    position: 'bottom-right'
  });
</script>
